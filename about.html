About


I have always liked creating things, preferably in the creative corner. That is why I have chosen to study frontend. There I can design a great and presentable website or app for the benefit of the users. Combining colors, composition and fonts to get a simple and user-friendly product is important. It should not be too complicated, but simple and stylish. It's so great to see the finished product after many hours of work, then you get real self-achievement!



My interest in computers


I think that when you work in data it is very good to be interested in the subject. I have always been interested in technology and especially in my childhood I started collecting old computers that people would throw away. Either I got them for free or I paid a small sum for them, but today they are worth a pretty big sum! They have more of an affective value for me where I think it is important to take care of historical things. Computer technology develops quickly and it is important to be up to date with the latest fashion!

Some of the computers from my computer collection. The oldest date back to 1978!

HTML, CSS and Javascript are the main program languages in front-end dev.
